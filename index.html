Location 1: British Columbia

British Columbia is a province of breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and vibrant cities. The towering Mountains offer amazing views to tourist. BC is also known as the home of Islands. Visitors can explore Vancouver, a dynamic city known for its cultural diversity and outdoor adventures. Whether it's hiking, swimming or food, British Columbia has something for everyone.

Location 3: Dubai

A skyline filled with glorious skyscrapers, luxury shopping, and rich culture: Dubai is an paragon of all this and much more! The Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, offers best views, while the man-made island which is known as Palm Jumeirah is a wonder to new tourist. Visitors can wander through the old Al Fahidi district to appreciate traditional life in the Emirates. Whether you're looking for a desert safari or modern attractions, there is something for everyone in Dubai.
